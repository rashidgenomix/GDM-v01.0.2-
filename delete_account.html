How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Germplasm Data Management account and all associated data, please email your request to:

toolsscience@gmail.com

Use the subject line: “Account Deletion Request – Germplasm Data Management”.

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered email address (the one you used to sign in to Germplasm Data Management).
  • Optional: brief confirmation that you understand data will be permanently deleted.

What Happens Next

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  1. Verify your account ownership.
  2. Delete your authentication record from Firebase Authentication.
  3. Delete all associated research and experiment data from Firebase Cloud Firestore.

You will receive confirmation by email once deletion is complete (typically within 7 days).

Data Retention

All your personal and research data will be permanently deleted from our Firebase databases. No data is retained unless legally required (for example, anonymized usage statistics or backup logs).
