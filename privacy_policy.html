1. Introduction

GermplasmX (“the App”) is developed by Plant Science Tools to support plant research and data management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects limited data necessary for its core functions:

  • Email Address – collected via Firebase Authentication for user login.
  • User ID – a unique identifier automatically created by Firebase to manage your account.
  • Research and Experiment Data – user-input data or CSV uploads saved securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore.

The app does not collect location data, contact lists, payment data, or analytics identifiers.

3. Permissions Used

  • Camera Permission – used only for scanning QR codes to facilitate data entry and quick access within the app. No images or videos are captured, stored, or shared.
  • File Access Permission – used to import and export CSV files for research data management. Files remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

No other sensitive permissions (e.g., microphone, contacts, location) are requested or used.

4. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and manage user accounts.
  • To securely store and retrieve user-entered or uploaded data.
  • To enable CSV import/export and QR code scanning features.

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing or analytics.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is securely stored using Google Firebase infrastructure and protected with encryption in transit (HTTPS). Access is restricted to authenticated users only.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your authentication record and associated data. You may also request data deletion by contacting toolsscience@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase for authentication, database, and cloud storage. Refer to their policy here: Firebase Privacy Policy.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Future Updates

If the App integrates new services (e.g., ads or analytics) in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified through the App and Play Store listing.
